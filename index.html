about
us

First St | R C is not your typical running crew. Founded in October 2022 by Natalia, Jaime and Cynthia - residents of a First St apartment block in Melbourne's Inner West, began this humble endeavour to transcend language and cultural barriers. Soon this grass roots run club grew into an eclectic crew of runners from varying nationalities, languages, professions and abilities spanning the 3012 postcode. United by their passion for running and their collective power to shape their community, running became not just an activity but a way to activate and cultivate a sense of community. We're a bunch of like-minded movers and shakers, committed to the act of running as a vehicle for connection and change. Come one...come all!

mission

Reshape the lives of inner-city communities through passion, collective power and accessible running experiences.

vision

Equip inner-city communities to be active, through shared knowledge, solidarity and considered running experiences.

position

First St | R C is a transformative initiative, led by community organisers to create and promote a transparent experience towards a healthier life.
